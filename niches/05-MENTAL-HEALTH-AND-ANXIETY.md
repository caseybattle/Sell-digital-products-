# Niche 5: Mental Health & Anxiety Management

**Target Audience:** Women aged 22-38 dealing with anxiety, overthinking, and burnout
**Core Pain Point:** Stuck in a cycle of overthinking, people-pleasing, and burnout. Can't "turn off" their brain at night, feel guilty for resting. Can't afford therapy or on waitlists.

---

## #1: "The Calm Brain Toolkit"
**Format:** Notion/PDF Template Bundle + Short Audio Guides
**Price:** $27-$37

**What to Include:**
- "Brain Dump to Clarity" structured journaling template (guided CBT prompts, not blank pages)
- "Thought Court" worksheet — put anxious thoughts "on trial" using evidence-for/against columns
- 5 pre-built evening wind-down routines (each under 15 min) as one-page checklists
- "Sunday Reset for Anxious Brains" planning template with energy budgeting
- "Permission to Rest" self-care menu — micro-rest options by time (2 min, 10 min, 30 min)
- 3 short audio walkthroughs (5-7 min each)

**Why It Sells:** The trigger is *immediate relief*. They don't want a 6-week course — they want something for tonight. "Toolkit" signals practical and ready-to-use. "Thought Court" is novel enough to be screenshot-worthy on Instagram, driving organic shares.

**Ease of Creation:** Very high — build in a weekend with Canva and Notion.
**Income Potential:** High volume front-end product.

## #2: "The Overthinking Detox — 7-Day Audio Challenge"
**Format:** Private podcast feed or course platform (audio-first + PDF worksheets)
**Price:** $47

**What to Include:**
- Day 1: "Why Your Brain Won't Shut Up" — normalizing the loop, simple CBT model
- Day 2: "The Stories You Tell Yourself" — identifying cognitive distortions with inventory worksheet
- Day 3: "The People-Pleasing Trap" — boundary-setting scripts and guilt cycle
- Day 4: "Body-First Calm" — somatic techniques when cognitive tools feel like too much
- Day 5: "The Guilt-Free Rest Day" — reframing rest as productive
- Day 6: "Building Your Anxiety First-Aid Kit" — personalized coping plan
- Day 7: "Relapse-Proofing Your Brain" — what to do when the spiral returns
- Each day: 10-15 min audio + one fillable worksheet

**Why It Sells:** The trigger is *structured hope with a deadline*. "7 days" feels achievable. Audio format is critical — reading a 40-page ebook feels like homework. Audio feels like a calm friend talking them through it. Gets shared in Instagram DMs constantly.

## #3: "Anxious Girl's Boundaries Playbook"
**Format:** Designed ebook/digital guide (Canva, 40-60 pages, highly visual)
**Price:** $29

**What to Include:**
- Why anxious people struggle with boundaries (neuroscience explained simply)
- 4 boundary types you're violating with yourself (time, energy, emotional, digital)
- 25 copy-paste scripts by scenario: saying no at work, declining social plans, guilt-tripping family, limits with partner, handling "you've changed"
- "Boundary Builder" framework — step-by-step decision tree for new situations
- Managing guilt after setting a boundary — specific CBT techniques
- Bonus: "Boundary Audit" self-assessment quiz

**Why It Sells:** The trigger is *borrowed courage*. Pre-written scripts eliminate the executive function barrier. "Anxious Girl's" creates immediate identity resonance. Most shareable product — people tag friends, screenshot scripts, post on stories.

## #4: "From Burnout to Balanced — Self-Paced Recovery Course"
**Format:** Self-paced mini-course (video/audio modules + worksheets + community)
**Price:** $97-$147

**What to Include:**
- Module 1: The Burnout Map — identify where you are on the spectrum (overcommitment → exhaustion → depersonalization → collapse)
- Module 2: The Energy Audit — track where energy goes for one week
- Module 3: Rebuilding Identity Beyond Productivity — CBT work on "I'm only valuable when I'm useful"
- Module 4: The Minimum Viable Day — sustainable daily structure based on energy, not ambition
- Module 5: Relational Burnout — how people-pleasing drains you
- Module 6: 90-Day Sustainability Plan
- Bonus: Monthly live Q&A or private community

**Why It Sells:** The trigger is *identity transformation*. "Recovery" validates that what they're experiencing is real and serious. Self-paced format is essential — adding deadlines to a burned-out person is counterproductive. "Minimum Viable Day" becomes a branded talking point.

## #5: "The Anxiety Companion — Monthly Membership"
**Format:** Recurring membership (Patreon, course platform, or private podcast feed)
**Price:** $12-$18/month

**What to Include:**
- 1 new themed worksheet pack per month (Jan = "New Year Without Pressure," Mar = "Spring Cleaning Thought Patterns")
- 2 new guided audio sessions per month (mindfulness + cognitive restructuring)
- 1 "Anxiety Toolkit Update" — new technique/script/framework monthly
- Monthly live session or AMA audio drop
- Growing archive of all past content

**Why It Sells:** The trigger is *ongoing companionship*. Anxiety isn't a one-time fix. "Less than one coffee a week for your mental health." Deepest relationship builder — turns customers into community.

**At 300 members x $15/month = $4,500/month recurring.**
