# Niche 2: Personal Finance & Budgeting

**Target Audience:** Young adults aged 22-35 living paycheck to paycheck, especially with student loan debt
**Core Pain Point:** Overwhelmed by money management, don't know where their money goes, feel ashamed about their financial situation

---

## #1: "The Paycheck Reset" — Monthly Budget Spreadsheet Kit
**Format:** Google Sheets / Excel Template Bundle
**Price:** $27

**What to Include:**
- Pre-built monthly budget spreadsheet with auto-calculating categories (rent, groceries, subscriptions, debt payments, "fun money")
- "Money Map" tab with auto-generated pie charts showing where every dollar goes
- Bi-weekly paycheck splitter (for people paid every two weeks, not monthly)
- Debt snowball tracker with projected payoff date that updates automatically
- "Subscriptions Audit" tab tallying forgotten recurring charges
- 1-page PDF quick-start guide: "Set This Up in 12 Minutes"

**Why It Sells:** The trigger is *immediate control*. Their core pain is "I don't know where my money goes." A clean, color-coded spreadsheet makes them feel like they finally have their life together. Most Instagrammable product — people screenshot their filled-in budget as a flex.

**Income Potential:** Very high. Zero fulfillment cost, sells on autopilot. 100 sales/month = $2,700/month passive.
**Ease of Creation:** 1-2 weekends.

## #2: "Broke to Breathing Room" — The 30-Day Money Detox Guide
**Format:** PDF Guide (40-50 pages) + Companion Checklist
**Price:** $37

**What to Include:**
- Day 1-7: "The Audit" — track every transaction, identify 3 biggest money leaks
- Day 8-14: "The Cut" — scripts for canceling subscriptions, negotiating bills (with exact phone numbers)
- Day 15-21: "The System" — automatic transfers, build $500 emergency buffer using micro-transfers
- Day 22-30: "The Maintenance" — weekly 15-minute money check-in ritual
- Bonus: "The Shame-Free Money Talk" — scripts for talking to partners/family about money
- Printable daily tracker sheets

**Why It Sells:** The trigger is *a finite timeline with a promise of relief*. "Breathing room" is the exact emotional outcome they want — not being rich, just stopping the panic on the 28th.

**Pairs with Product #1 as a bundle at $47.**

## #3: "Student Loan Game Plan" — Debt Payoff Strategy Kit
**Format:** Mini-Course (video + worksheets) via Gumroad or Stan Store
**Price:** $67

**What to Include:**
- Module 1: "Know Your Enemy" — pull exact loan details, understand interest types, calculate true cost
- Module 2: "Pick Your Weapon" — avalanche vs. snowball with real math using $38K-$55K salaries
- Module 3: "Income-Based Repayment Decoder" — plain-English walkthrough of IBR, PAYE, SAVE plans
- Module 4: "The Payoff Accelerator" — one extra payment per quarter strategy
- Module 5: "The Forgiveness Question" — PSLF eligibility checker and timeline calculator
- Downloadable worksheets + loan payoff calculator spreadsheet

**Why It Sells:** The trigger is *fear reduction through specificity*. "You will be debt-free by March 2031" instead of "it depends." Student loan content goes viral consistently on Instagram Reels.

## #4: "The $500 Emergency Fund Challenge" — Micro-Savings System
**Format:** Printable PDF Kit + Email Drip Sequence
**Price:** $17

**What to Include:**
- Visual "savings thermometer" printable for fridge or mirror
- 30 daily micro-challenges (Transfer $5, sell one closet item, cook instead of ordering, call insurance for loyalty discount)
- Each challenge has an estimated dollar amount showing progress compounding
- "Quick Wins Rolodex" — 25 ways to find $10-$50 this week without a side hustle
- Celebration tracker: milestones at $100, $250, $500
- Optional daily email/text reminders

**Why It Sells:** The trigger is *achievability*. $500 doesn't feel impossible. It's the difference between a flat tire being an inconvenience vs. a crisis.

**Best as a funnel product** that leads to Products #1, #2, and #3.
