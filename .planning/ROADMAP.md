# Roadmap: The Reset Method — Digital Products Business

## Overview

From existing Gumroad products to a fully automated $10K+/month digital products business. Phase 1 focuses on the April 2026 tax refund window — maximum organic reach while the audience has money. Phase 2 builds the automation layer (n8n, GoHighLevel). Phase 3 scales with paid traffic and the MRR agency play.
