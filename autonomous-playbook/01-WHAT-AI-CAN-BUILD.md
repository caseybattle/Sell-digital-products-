## What AI Genuinely CANNOT Do

Be honest about this:

- **Video with a human face** — No workaround that doesn't feel fake
- **Original photography** — AI image generators supplement but aren't reliable for brand consistency
- **Quality filtering** — AI generates volume; a human decides what's actually good
- **Platform assembly** — AI writes content; a human still clicks buttons in Notion, Canva, Gumroad
- **Market positioning** — AI suggests; the human makes the final strategic call

**The bottom line:** AI is a complete back-end content engine. Your remaining role is **curator, assembler, and publisher** — not creator.
