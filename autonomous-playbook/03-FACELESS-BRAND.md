## Account Setup (Day 1)

### Name Formula
| Formula | Example |
|---|---|
| [Niche] + Vault | TheMoneyVault |
| [Niche] + Playbook | WealthPlaybook |
| The + [Niche] + Hub | TheProductivityHub |
| Daily + [Niche] | DailyMindReset |

Pick 5 names. Check availability on Instagram, TikTok, Gumroad, and .com simultaneously. Secure the same handle everywhere.

### Bio Structure
```
Line 1: What the account IS
Line 2: What followers GET
Line 3: Social proof or specificity
Line 4: CTA + link
```

**Example:**
```
The Reset Method
Systems, templates & tools to fix your life in 15 minutes a day
Join 1,200+ people who stopped overthinking ↓
[Gumroad/Stan Store link]
```

### Visual Identity
- **3 colors only.** One dominant, one accent, one neutral.
- **2 fonts only.** One bold for headlines, one clean for body.
- **Profile pic:** Bold monogram or letter on brand color. NOT a face.
- **5 Story highlights:** Start Here, Free Resource, Products, Results, Tips

## Content That Works Without a Face

### Format 1: Text-on-Screen Reels (4-5x/week)
7-15 second videos. Text appears in sequence. Trending audio underneath.
```
Hook (0-2s): "Stop doing [common mistake]"
Problem (2-5s): "Most people [pain point]"
Solution (5-10s): "Instead, try [method]"
CTA (10-13s): "Save this + follow for more"
```
**Tools:** Canva Animate → export as MP4, or CapCut text templates + stock footage.

### Format 2: Carousel Posts (2-3x/week)
7-10 swipeable slides. Highest saves and shares.
```
Slide 1: Bold hook + "Swipe →"
Slides 2-8: One point per slide, big text
Slide 9: Summary
Slide 10: "Follow @handle for more" or "Free guide in bio"
```

### Format 3: Quote Graphics (1-2x/week)
Centered bold text on brand-colored background.
- "You don't need more followers. You need a better offer."
- "A $27 template that saves 10 hours is worth $270."

### Format 4: Screenshot-Style Posts (1-2x/week)
Styled as tweets, notes app screenshots, or DM conversations.

### Format 5: Stock Footage Reels
Free footage from Pexels/Pixabay + text overlay + trending audio.

## The AI Batch Workflow: 30 Days of Content in One Sitting

**Step 1:** Ask AI for 30 content ideas (10 carousels, 10 Reels, 5 quotes, 5 screenshots)

**Step 2:** Ask AI to write full copy for all 30 (carousel text, Reel scripts, captions)

**Step 3:** Paste into Canva templates. Use Bulk Create for quotes.

**Step 4:** Take Reel scripts into CapCut or InVideo AI → generate videos.

**Step 5:** Load into Later or Buffer. Schedule the entire month.

**Total time: 6-10 hours for 30 days of content.**

## Selling in Stories Without Your Face

### The Daily Sales Engine (3-4x/week)
```
Story 1: State pain point (text on brand background)
  "Still spending hours on a budget that doesn't work?"

Story 2: Agitate
  "That's 10+ hours/month you're losing to spreadsheet confusion."

Story 3: Solution
  "What if your budget auto-calculated everything in 12 minutes?"

Story 4: Product (screenshot/mockup + link sticker)
  "This is what I built. Link below."

Story 5: Social proof
  [Screenshot of testimonial or download count]
```

### Other Faceless Story Types
- **Text-only value tips** on brand-colored backgrounds
- **Polls and quizzes** for engagement and market research
- **Countdown stickers** for launches (free push notifications)
- **Screen recordings** of your product in action

## Building Trust Without a Face

1. **Product quality as proof** — Show detailed previews and screen recordings
2. **Volume of social proof** — Aggressively collect testimonials, screenshot every positive DM
3. **Data and specificity** — "Downloaded 2,300+ times" beats "lots of people love this"
4. **Consistency** — 3 months of daily posts builds more trust than any personal brand
5. **Professional design** — Your feed IS your credibility
6. **Own it** — "No face. No fluff. Just systems that work."

---

## The "Curator" Positioning

Don't claim to be an expert. Claim to be the person who **organized** expert knowledge.

**In your bio:** "I research [topic] so you don't have to."

**In captions:** "I spent 40 hours testing productivity systems so I could distill them into one template."

**In product descriptions:** "This isn't theory. This is a curated collection of what's actually working right now."

This is honest, defensible, humble, and it sells.
