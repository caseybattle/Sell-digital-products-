## The Recommended 2-Platform Strategy

For maximum autopilot, use TWO platforms:

### Platform 1: Gumroad (Your Home Base)
- **Why:** API for product creation, built-in Discover marketplace, handles payments/delivery/taxes/emails, 10% only-when-you-sell (no monthly fee)
- **Use for:** All products — spreadsheets, PDFs, Notion templates, ebooks
- **Set up once:** Create product, upload files, write description, set price, enable Discover. Walk away.

### Platform 2: Etsy OR Amazon KDP (Your Traffic Engine)
- **Why:** Built-in marketplace with millions of active buyers searching for products like yours
- **Etsy** if selling: templates, printables, planners, checklists, worksheets
- **Amazon KDP** if selling: ebooks, journals, planners, guides
- **The key insight:** These platforms have buyers already looking. You don't need to drive traffic.

### What to AVOID for autonomous selling:
- **Teachable, Stan Store, Shopify** — High monthly fees that bleed money if sales are slow
- **AppSumo** — Requires active partnership and customer support
- **Anything requiring live delivery** — Cohorts, coaching, memberships need ongoing work

## The Zero-Touch Delivery Flow

```
Customer finds product (Gumroad Discover / Etsy search / your IG link)
         ↓
Customer pays (Stripe/PayPal — handled by platform)
         ↓
Platform sends download link automatically
         ↓
Customer gets product instantly
         ↓
Platform handles receipt email, tax collection, refunds
         ↓
Money hits your account (weekly/biweekly payout)
         ↓
You do nothing.
```

**Total ongoing effort after setup: ZERO.**

The only thing you add over time is new products and marketing content — both of which AI generates.
