## TIER 1: Set Up Once, Runs Forever

### Pinterest (THE most underrated channel for digital products)

**Why Pinterest is the #1 autopilot traffic source:**
- Pinterest is a SEARCH ENGINE, not a social network. Pins rank for keywords and drive traffic for 6-12+ months
- 80%+ of Pinterest users are in "buying mode" — they're searching for solutions
- Digital product niches (budgeting, productivity, self-care, planners) dominate Pinterest

**AI automation:**
- AI writes pin titles and descriptions optimized for Pinterest SEO
- AI generates 20-50 pin descriptions per product in one session
- Canva creates pin graphics from templates (vertical 1000x1500px)
- Tailwind or Pinterest's native scheduler auto-posts 5-15 pins/day

**Strategy:**
- Create 10-20 different pin designs per product (same product, different visuals/angles)
- Each pin targets a different keyword
- Schedule 3-6 months of pins in advance using Tailwind
- Every pin links directly to your Gumroad product page

**Keyword examples per niche:**
- Finance: "budget spreadsheet template," "paycheck budgeting method," "debt payoff tracker"
- Mental Health: "anxiety journal prompts," "CBT worksheets free," "boundary setting scripts"
- Productivity: "notion dashboard template," "solopreneur planner," "business SOP template"
- Dating: "attachment style quiz," "dating red flags checklist," "relationship journal"

**After setup effort: 15 minutes/month** (review analytics, adjust what's working)

### Email Marketing (the highest-converting channel)

**The setup (one-time):**
1. Choose platform: **MailerLite** (free up to 1,000 subscribers, full automation)
2. Create a free lead magnet (AI writes a mini-version of your paid product)
3. AI writes your entire email sequence once:
   - 7-email welcome sequence
   - 5-email post-purchase upsell sequence
   - 3-email abandoned cart sequence
4. Set up automation triggers in MailerLite
5. Never touch it again

**The welcome sequence (AI writes all 7):**

| Email | Timing | Purpose |
|-------|--------|---------|
| 1 | Immediate | Deliver lead magnet + introduce yourself |
| 2 | Day 2 | Share one tactical tip (build trust) |
| 3 | Day 4 | Tell a story connecting to your product |
| 4 | Day 6 | Soft pitch — "I made something for this" |
| 5 | Day 8 | Testimonial/social proof email |
| 6 | Day 10 | Objection handling — address "why should I buy" |
| 7 | Day 12 | Final pitch with urgency |

**The post-purchase upsell (AI writes all 5):**

| Email | Timing | Purpose |
|-------|--------|---------|
| 1 | Immediate | Thank you + quick start guide |
| 2 | Day 3 | "How's it going?" + one bonus tip |
| 3 | Day 7 | "Your [niche] is handled. But what about [adjacent niche]?" |
| 4 | Day 10 | Cross-sell story + testimonial |
| 5 | Day 14 | Direct pitch for next product |

**After setup effort: ZERO.** Emails send themselves based on triggers.

### Blog/SEO Content

**Platform:** Start with **Substack** (free, built-in audience, SEO-indexed) or a simple **WordPress** site

**Strategy:**
- AI writes 2-4 long-form articles per month (1,500-2,500 words each)
- Target long-tail keywords with buyer intent
- Each article naturally links to your product as the solution
- Articles compound over time — a post written today still drives traffic in 12 months

**Article types that convert to sales:**
- "How to [solve problem] in [timeframe]" → links to your template/tool
- "The [X] best [tools/methods] for [audience]" → your product is #1
- "[Problem] vs [Problem]: Which is killing your [goal]?" → your product solves both
- "[X]-step guide to [desired outcome]" → your product is the shortcut

**Long-tail keywords per niche (low competition, buyer intent):**

**Finance:**
- "budget spreadsheet for beginners google sheets"
- "how to budget on irregular income template"
- "paycheck to paycheck budget planner printable"
- "debt snowball calculator spreadsheet free"

**Mental Health:**
- "CBT thought record worksheet printable"
- "anxiety journal prompts for adults"
- "setting boundaries with family scripts"
- "evening routine for anxious mind checklist"

**Productivity:**
- "notion template for solopreneurs"
- "small business SOP template free"
- "solopreneur daily planning system"
- "how to create standard operating procedures small business"

**Dating:**
- "attachment style workbook"
- "toxic relationship pattern quiz"
- "what to text after first date scripts"
- "how to stop self sabotaging relationships"

**After setup effort: 2-3 hours/month** (AI writes articles, you review and post)

## TIER 2: Low Maintenance, Good Returns

### Twitter/X Threads
- AI writes 3-5 threads per week. Each thread is 5-10 tweets of value, last tweet links to product
- Schedule with Typefully or Buffer
- Threads get bookmarked and reshared for weeks
- **Maintenance: 30 min/week** (AI writes, you schedule)

### Medium / Substack Articles
- AI writes 1-2 articles per week
- Medium's Partner Program pays you AND drives traffic to your product
- Substack builds an email list simultaneously
- **Maintenance: 1-2 hours/week**

### Affiliate/Referral System
- Gumroad has built-in affiliate features
- Offer 25-30% commission to other creators who promote your product
- They drive traffic. You pay only on sales. Zero risk.
- **Maintenance: ZERO** after setup

## TIER 3: Higher Effort, Higher Reward

### Faceless YouTube (optional but powerful)
- AI writes scripts → ElevenLabs generates voiceover → stock footage + text overlays
- Types that work: "5 Budget Mistakes Keeping You Broke" (tips), screen recordings of templates
- Takes 2-3 hours per video but YouTube videos drive traffic for YEARS
- **Only do this after Tier 1 channels are running**

### Quora / Reddit
- AI drafts thoughtful, valuable answers to questions in your niche
- Link to your free lead magnet (NOT directly to product — communities hate that)
- Reddit is risky (bans promotional accounts) — use sparingly and add genuine value
- **Maintenance: 1 hour/week**

## The "Publish Once, Earn Forever" Content Map

To reach critical mass and generate consistent passive traffic:

| Content Type | Quantity Needed | Traffic Kicks In |
|-------------|----------------|-----------------|
| Pinterest pins | 50-100 pins total | 2-4 weeks |
| Blog/Substack articles | 15-20 articles | 2-3 months |
| Twitter/X threads | 30+ threads | 1-2 weeks |
| Email sequences | 15 emails (set once) | Immediately |
| Gumroad/Etsy listings | 3-5 products | 1-2 weeks |

**The magic number: ~100 pieces of content across platforms.**

After 100 pieces are live, you have a traffic flywheel that sends people to your products every day without you doing anything new. Each new product or content piece just adds fuel.

## The 15-Minute Weekly Check-In (Your ONLY Recurring Task)

After the initial 2-week setup, your weekly maintenance is:

1. **5 min:** Check Gumroad/Etsy dashboard for sales and reviews
2. **5 min:** Check email subscriber growth and open rates
3. **5 min:** Glance at Instagram/Pinterest analytics — what's getting saves?

That's it. Everything else is automated or batched monthly.

**Monthly batch session (2-3 hours):**
- Ask AI to generate next month's content batch
- Schedule it all in one sitting
- Review and respond to any customer emails/DMs
