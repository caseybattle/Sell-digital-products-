## The 5 Agents in the Pack

### Agent 1: Budget Coach Claude
System prompt that turns Claude into a personal CFO.
- Analyzes your spending when you paste in your bank statement
- Finds patterns, identifies leaks, creates a monthly reset plan
- Asks the right questions to understand your full financial picture
- Gives action steps ranked by impact

### Agent 2: Boundary Builder Claude
System prompt that turns Claude into a script-writing coach for hard conversations.
- You describe the situation, it writes word-for-word scripts
- Explains WHY each script works (the psychology)
- Offers variations for different levels of assertiveness
- Handles follow-up scenarios ("what if they say X back?")

### Agent 3: Calm Brain Coach Claude
System prompt for anxiety and overthinking management.
- Does Thought Court CBT sessions with you
- Guides you through brain dumps and helps you find clarity
- Evening check-in mode for night-time spirals
- Sunday Reset planning sessions

### Agent 4: Paycheck Planner Claude
System prompt for weekly/bi-weekly money management.
- Split your paycheck automatically (you tell it your bills, it tells you the plan)
- Tracks whether you're on pace for your savings goal
- Flags unusual spending patterns you describe
- Suggests specific moves for debt payoff

### Agent 5: Reset Strategist Claude (Bonus)
Big-picture life reset coach.
- Helps you audit every area of your life (money, health, relationships, work)
- Creates 90-day reset plans
- Weekly accountability check-in prompts
- Helps you identify and break recurring patterns
