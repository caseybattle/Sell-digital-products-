## CONVERTING MD FILES TO PDF (FREE)

Option A: **Google Docs** — Open file, paste content, File → Download → PDF
Option B: **Canva** — Paste content into a Doc template, customize design, download PDF
Option C: **Pandoc** (if you have it): `pandoc file.md -o file.pdf`

For the best-looking PDFs, use Canva with a clean template. Paste the text, match it to your brand colors (navy + gold), and export. The visual quality is what justifies the price.
