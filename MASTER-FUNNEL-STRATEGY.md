## A. THE UMBRELLA BRAND CONCEPT

### Brand Name: **"The Reset Method"**

**Unified Pain Point:** *"I'm stuck in a loop — doing the same things, getting the same results, and I don't know how to break the cycle."*

Every product is a "Reset" for a different area of life:
- The 15-Minute Body **Reset** (Fitness)
- The Paycheck **Reset** (Finance)
- The Attachment Style **Reset** (Relationships)
- The Solopreneur Operating System (Productivity **Reset**)
- The Calm Brain Toolkit (Mental **Reset**)

**Why this works:** The word "Reset" implies:
1. You're not broken — you just need to restart with a better system
2. It's fast (a reset, not a rebuild)
3. It's empowering (you're taking control)

**Target Mega-Audience:** Overwhelmed, ambitious 25-40 year olds who are high-functioning on the outside but feel like they're falling apart on the inside. They're the "looks like they have it together" crowd who privately Google things at 11pm.

**Brand Voice:** Direct, empathetic, zero fluff. Talks like a smart friend who's 2 years ahead of you, not a guru on a mountain.
